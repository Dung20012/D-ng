A website (also written as a web site) is one or more web pages and related content that is
identified by a commom domain name and published on at least one web server. Websites are
typically dedicated to a particular topic purpose, such as news, education, commerce,
entertainment, or social media. Hyperlinking between web pages guides the navigation of the
site, which often starts with a home page. The most-visited sites are Google, YouTube, and
Facebook.

All publicly-accessible websites collectively constitute the World Wide Web. There are also
private websites that can only be accessed on a private netword, such as a company's interrnal
websites for its employees. Users can access websites on a range of devices, including
desktops, laptop, tablets, and smartphones. The app used on these devices is called a web
brower.
